XML2RFC Internet Draft Creation Wizard


This XML2RFC Internet Draft Creation Wizard is designed to help you create a new internet draft using the XML2RFC XML directives. The XML is constantly displayed at the bottom, and all changes are immediately reflected there.

In the following pages, you will be asked a series of questions about your draft.
  • Any answers that are required will appear as a box with a solid border, as in: .
  • Any answers that are optional will appear as a box with a dotted border, as in: .
  • You may click on to get more information about specific questions. If you click it right now, you will find information on where the instructional material has been derived.
Click Create a New Internet Draft to continue.

XML2RFC Wizard: Last Page


Congratulations!

Your XML2RFC document is now complete and it's now your turn to fill in the rest of the blanks. Either copy and paste the document from below, or click Download a Copy to save to a file.

After downloading the XML file, edit the XML using your favorite XML editing tools. A description of various tools that you can use to help edit your document can be found on the Author Tools and IETF Tools pages.

To convert your XML to an internet draft, follow the directions found on the XML2RFC resource page.

To upload your new internet draft into the Internet Drafts repository, use the I-D Upload page.
